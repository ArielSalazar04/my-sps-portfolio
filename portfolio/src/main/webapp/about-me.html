About Me

Background

I was born and raised in New York my entire life, and I am also of Mexican descent. I can speak fluent English and Spanish, which is something that I value because I enjoy making connections with people from both English and Spanish-speaking communities.

I consider myself a hard-working student with hopes and dreams of obtaining a position in a software or machine learning engineering career. I also consider myself a laid-back and friendly person in both my academic and social lives. Although I am competitive, I strive to be supportive of my peers and help those who are in need. I believe that having a positive mental attitude is one of the essentials to becoming a better person overall, and this is why I apply this rule to my endeavors and encourage others to do the same.

Apart from school, playing billiards and doing parkour are a couple of my other hobbies. I enjoy playing billiards because it is my way of relieving the stress and burden of my academic and personal life. Although I do not consistently practice parkour in the same way I did during high school, I practice it occasionally to stay fit. Both of these sports have allowed me to maintain good self-esteem and to clear my mind when I need to take a break. Both billiards and parkour continue to play a significant role in my life overall.
