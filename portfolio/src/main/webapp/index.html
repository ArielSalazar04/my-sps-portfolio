Welcome To My Portfolio!

My name is Ariel Salazar. I am currently a junior in college with computer science and mathematics as my major and minor fields of study. While I have a passion for logic, mathematics, and problem-solving, it has taken me a lot of time and effort to build the skills that I have today.

As I approached my senior year in high school, I was highly motivated to enroll in the only computer science course that my school offered. However, I did not get a chance to take it because the computer science program was small and, therefore, the course did not run during my year. Nevertheless, I did not lose my motivation, and I chose computer science as my major when I applied to college. Although I did not have any coding experience before college, I learned a lot during my freshman year, and I quickly became engaged with the field of study overall. I invested several hours each day practicing what I learned, and I continuously looked forward to attending the next lecture.

As of today, computer science is one of my hobbies rather than just an ordinary school subject. I dedicate a lot of time learning new things every day and becoming more experienced as a programmer. I strive to do my best, never give up without a fight, and to encourage others to do the same.
